Tokaji Wine

In addition to Sherry wine, there are several worthy and original fortified wines that I would like to discuss. They are Port wine, Madeira wine, and Tokaji wine.

Let's start with Tokaji — this wine can launch transformations of great power, and it is vital to cope with them.

Why are such transformations possible? The secret is simple — ambition. Ambition is the fuel for all changes. When the wild energy of ambition is applied to hard work, it is transformed into success. This process is connected with time, money, values, and deals. Knowing your values, being able to identify other people’s values and close mutually beneficial deals…. Desire to care for and to achieve prosperity. Anything on the scale between greediness and becoming a source of material benefits to others.

Tokaji wine can give a person without ambitions some acceleration. Naturally, ambitious people may go off balance and lose touch with reality and the feelings and needs of those around them.

Vinum Regum, Rex Vinorum. The king of wines, the wine for kings. (King Louis XIV of France)

Tokaji wine is about being at the top of the world: the best, king, and supreme. But beware. “The Greatest Showman” movie, for example, is, among other things, a film about the power of ambition. The protagonist drastically changed his life and the lives of many but almost lost everything when he got carried away by his ambitions. This is a pure Tokaji theme: how to bring transformation and progress to the world without ruining things that matter?
