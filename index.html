The idea of mindful alcohol use

In the hands of a reasonable person, any poison can become medicine. All the stories in this book are like a miniature educational program and my contribution to the mindful drinking of alcohol and delivering a person from pathological dependence on anything.

It is known by specialists, who deal with substance abuse in rehabs, that every person eventually chooses a specific substance and clings to it. Thus the addiction is formed. Such substance provides psycho-emotional states that a person cannot reach in real life. Knowing the essence of every substance makes it easy to understand what is missing in life. It may become a starting point for further changes. The only reliable source of knowledge regarding the essence of substances is homeopathy.

Homeopathy (from Greek ὅμοιος — "similar" and πάθος — "disease") is an excellent source of information about elements, minerals, plants, insects, animals, and more and their impact on human health.

Homeopathy is a type of medicine. In homeopathy, highly diluted preparations are used, the active substance of which causes symptoms in healthy people similar to those of the patient's illness. The treatment concept is based on “like cures like” (lat. similia similibus curantur).

Numerous tinctures are used for medicinal purposes, each containing alcohol as a carrier agent. We do not abuse them but consume them with great care. This book is an attempt to take a similar look at alcoholic beverages we consume recreationally and to learn how they affect us psychologically.

So, to avoid alcohol abuse, one should be aware of the effects that different alcoholic beverages may offer and consume with understanding and mindfulness.

What is the connection between alcoholic drinks and homeopathy?

Potency and Similarity

Alcohol potentializes everything that gets into it, meaning it helps every substance to take effect very quickly. As in homeopathy, the choice of a drink should be based on the principle of similarity: constituent elements of a drink should resonate with a person's psycho-emotional state.

The Essence

The essence of a drink comes from its ingredients. Each plant and each element generates certain psycho-emotional states in a person. This idea is not new, but most people have forgotten it. People tend to eat and drink, relying more on ads, trends, and marketing. No wonder we have to face a growing number of health issues. It is essential to understand that all good booze was created not by fools but by people who know the essence of plants and elements. This knowledge is present in homeopathy.

Enough is enough

The intake of any drink should be stopped at the very moment when the desired psycho-emotional state is reached. Otherwise, the effect will be the opposite, with many side effects. When one pays attention to the slightest changes that an alcoholic drink provokes in him, it is easy to notice when it is time to stop. This moment comes long before a person loses the sense of reality.
