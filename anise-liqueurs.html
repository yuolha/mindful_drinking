Anise Liqueurs

In addition to being great medicines, anise liqueurs give a person a sense of security and acceptance when appropriately used. Let us consider in more detail the essence of anises:

• Ordinary anise — Pimpinella anísum — is for those feeling like a scapegoat or outcast.
• Star anise — Illicíum vérum — resonates with birth trauma, a sense of threat and danger, defencelessness, and the loss of everything, as if all that is left is only your body and it is also in danger.

Anise liqueurs for those who lacked parental care and support in childhood, whose home was a constant, unsafe, and unpredictable battlefield.

If we talk specifically about Sambuca, then there is another theme that adds up to anise:

An extract from berries or flowers of elderberry — Sambucus nigra — is about unrequited love or love in which a person is afraid of losing their freedom; it’s about disappointment and loneliness.

Sambuca is a liqueur for those with relationship problems who find it difficult or impossible to build long-term relationships due to childhood trauma. For example, someone’s parents divorced, leading to disappointment in marriage and personal life.

In addition, Sambuca is very useful in treating acute respiratory infections, acute respiratory viral infections that develop against the background of problematic love relationships.
