Cream Liqueurs

Cream liqueurs have so many varieties. Every manufacturer tries to add their unique twist to the base idea of this drink, to make it appealing and memorable. So what is the main idea? What comes to your mind when you think of an Irish cream liqueur, for example? Coffee? A warm blanket, perhaps?

Cream (the fatty fraction of milk) in liqueurs gives a sense of safety, comfort, and your home’s coziness. It nourishes and brings back memories of one’s mother’s love. The cream provides stability, relaxation, and a good temper. It lowers the levels of aggression.

Let us see what famous brands add to enrich the drink.

Baileys Irish Cream contains:
• Irish whiskey — is all about personal power.
• Chocolate — Theobroma cacao — sadness and loneliness due to the loss of family; the family may have been abusive and withdrawn; a person in need of contact and warm relationships; artistic, not like everybody else, strange, living in a loving and soft fantasy world.
• Vanilla — Vanilla planifolia — well, this one is someone playful, with a strong desire for perfection, beauty, and spirituality, looking for harmony and happiness; on the flip side of it, there is criticism, depression, and a desire to be left alone and silent. Vanilla is about someone who needs to be grounded deeply in their roots, connected to the community, and busy bringing beauty to the world. It also shows the dilemma between truly being all those things and merely pretending.

Amarula Cream Liqueur stands out with the marula fruit — Sclerocarya birrea. The essence of the marula fruit describes a situation when a person needs to adapt to the hierarchy in family and/or society while trying to assimilate all the best qualities and discard the counterproductive ones.

Besides chocolate, vanilla, and whiskey, Sheridan’s Coffee Layered Liqueur offers us strong coffee vibes. Coffee Arabica is bright, intellectual, responsible, independent, ambitious, and ready to pursue goals. Idealistic, striving for perfection, artistic and creative. They may not feel valued or acknowledged for their deeds.

Some cream liqueurs may contain rum as a base liquor. 

Summing up, cream liqueurs are the drinks of a lost paradise. They are a gentle reminder that no matter what has already happened in life or what is yet to come, that sweet and soft feeling of grounding and security is always there within our being. We need to learn to let go of everything that needs to be let go and embrace all the good things and all the love we have for ourselves and the world.
