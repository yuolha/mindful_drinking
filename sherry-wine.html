Sherry Wine

It isn’t easy to talk about this kind of love. Even more difficult is to find it, and it is just a super task to meet a true connoisseur of sherry wine.

Sherry wine is a drink of a mature personality. A mature person can establish productive social contacts and perceive reality and people adequately and thoroughly. A mature person can satisfy their needs and take responsibility for their actions and life. Mature people are inclined to look for their soul mate because they understand the power of trust, devotion, and co-creation.

The sherry wine is a drink about that only and the highest love. Because at the end of the day, having experienced everything or almost everything in life, when you think about it, you understand that without true love, everything falls apart.

On Sherry: The destiny of a thousand generations is concentrated in each drop. If the care of the world overwhelm you, only taste it, pilgrim, and you will swear that heaven is on earth. (Pedro Antonio de Alarcon)

Sherry wine is a special kind of love. This is a spiritual kind of love. Do not confuse it with the Osho kind of love, which is nothing but sex without obligations. The longing for higher love is longing for the soul that is your perfect match. Like Yin and Yang, like the cause and the effect… that’s the kind of love that sherry wine is about.

It isn’t easy to find such love. Having met it, it is even more challenging to live with it with dignity.

Sherry Wine knows how to recognize THE LOVE. Sherry wine is a contemplation, a meditation about this love.
