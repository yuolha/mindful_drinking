Lager Beer

This beer is for those who want to stop overthinking. Sometimes it makes sense when the brain is overloaded, constantly multitasking without proper rest, and when there’s too much to juggle. Well, first of stop and rest, I would say, and then see if you need different means to help you “declutter” your mind.

When consumed regularly, lager beer has a powerful and adverse effect on cognitive function. One may find themself struggling with incoherent lumps of thoughts.

Hops (Humulus lupulus), one of beer’s essential components, is of the cannabis family. Regarding its effects on the human psyche, homeopathy has the following conclusion: hops represents a person in need to adapt and be something less than they are, in need to survive, too weak to stand up for themselves, having a soft and clouded mind, distorted perception, and, as a result, incorrect conclusions.

Lager beer may become a medicine for those doubting themselves or putting too much pressure on themselves if consumed occasionally and in small volumes. But if you are a fan of lager beer and it is a drink of your choice at all times, then you may experience the essence of hops at its fullest and accept that you are less than you are. I do believe that is not what you’re going for.
