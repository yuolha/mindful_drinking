Citrus Liqueurs

Citrus liqueurs have lemons and oranges as the prominent flavor: Limoncello, Triple Sec, Grand Marnier, Cointreau, and many others.

They vibrate with a need for perfection. This means being hard on yourself sometimes, controlling, and having no right to fail or complain. This means hard work and complicated relationships.

Lemon liqueurs are for those whose ambitions brought them to exhaustion. Lemons — Citrus limon — have a strong sense of responsibility and must always be occupied. The aspirations of lemon differ from the ones of Tokaji wine. The ambition of Tokaji wine is to rule others, to define and transform their lives as a monarch. Lemons aspire to get work done, to run a business.

Orange liqueurs are for those who lack joy and seek abundance and merriment. Sweet orange (Citrus sinensis) and bitter orange (Citrus aurantium) dissolve sadness and nervousness; they guide you to your inner Sun, the source of energy and cheer.
