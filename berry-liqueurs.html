Berry Liqueurs

Berry liqueurs are about heavy social baggage that prevents a person from moving on.
Berry liqueurs are about fantasies and hunger. Hunger is both at the level of the body and desires at the level of life experiences. Berry liqueurs are for those who never started to live, who have everything according to family tradition, “life made us this way.” Still, inside the depths of the subconscious are emotions and fantasies about the unlived life.

Berry liqueurs help cope with accumulated dissatisfaction, provide an opportunity to dream relaxedly, and see where these fantasies may lead. Perhaps some sane plan for life may be born out of these dreams.
