The Divine Wine

No one knows where the first glass of wine was produced. Scientists say it happened about 8 thousand years ago in Georgia, or Turkey, or Armenia, or Iran, or Azerbaijan... Indeed, the Phoenicians, Greeks, and Romans made the most significant input into the winery.

For ancient Hellenes, drinking wine was an art. They experimented a lot with serving methods and temperature. They added herbs and spices to achieve a unique flavor and aroma.

The Greeks invented aged wine to keep it longer. They did not encourage drunkenness at all. Feasts and holidays were the only days when the wine flew like a river. Just like the ancient Greeks, I oppose any abuse. So, let us dive into the essence of this drink and its various types.
